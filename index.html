Privacy Policy

We built OneTraxx to be fast, fair and respectful of your data. This policy explains exactly what we collect, why we collect it, and the control you have over it.

🏢
1. Who We Are

OneTraxx is a live racing app operated by the OneTraxx team. If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at info@onetraxx.com.

This Privacy Policy applies to the OneTraxx mobile application (Android and iOS) and the website at onetraxx.com.

📋
2. What We Collect

We only collect what is necessary to run the app and improve your experience.

  • Account data — email address and password (if you create an account). Phone number if you choose SMS sign-in. These are stored securely on our servers.
  • Profile data — display name and profile photo (optional). You control what you share.
  • Race data — distances covered, finish times, pace, and position during races. This powers your stats and race history.
  • Race replay frames — up to 200 position snapshots per race, stored to enable the post-race preview feature.
  • Device & usage data — app version, device OS, and basic crash logs collected anonymously to improve stability.
  • Guest users — only a temporary session name. No email, no account, no persistent data stored.
🚫
3. What We Do Not Collect
  • We do not collect or store real-time GPS coordinates. Race distance is tracked locally on your device.
  • We do not sell your data to third parties — ever.
  • We do not run advertising or share data with ad networks.
  • We do not access your contacts, calendar, or any data outside the permissions you explicitly grant.
  • We do not collect health or medical data. Calorie estimates displayed in the app are approximate figures for 70 kg body weight and are not medical advice.
⚙️
4. How We Use Your Data
  • To run the app — authenticate your account, save your race history, and display your profile to friends you race with.
  • To improve the app — anonymous crash logs and usage patterns help us fix bugs and build better features.
  • To enable social features — your display name and avatar are visible to other users in rooms you join.
  • To send notifications — race invites and friend requests, only if you have notifications enabled. You can disable these at any time in Settings.
📷
5. Camera & Photo Permissions

The app requests camera and photo library access only when you choose to set a profile picture. These permissions are optional — you can use the full app without granting them.

Photos are compressed, uploaded to our secure servers, and used solely to display your avatar to other racers. We do not scan, analyse, or share your photos.

You can revoke these permissions at any time in your device Settings → Apps → OneTraxx → Permissions.

🤝
6. Data Sharing

We use the following third-party services to operate the app. Each operates under their own privacy policies:

  • Cloud infrastructure — we use industry-standard cloud providers for database, authentication, and file storage. All data is hosted on secure, encrypted servers.
  • Expo / EAS (expo.dev) — app build and delivery platform. Does not have access to your user data.

We do not share your personal data with any other parties unless required by law.

🗄️
7. Data Retention

Your account data and race history are retained for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all personal data (profile, race records, avatar) is permanently deleted within 30 days.

Race replay frames are stored per race and may be retained for up to 90 days after a race ends for the preview feature, then automatically deleted.

⚖️
8. Your Rights

Regardless of where you are located, you have the right to:

  • Correction — update your name or email directly in the app under Profile → Edit.
  • Deletion — delete your account and all associated data via Profile → Settings → Delete Account, or by emailing info@onetraxx.com.
  • Objection — opt out of any non-essential data processing by contacting us.

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or California (USA), these rights are also protected under GDPR, UK GDPR, and CCPA respectively.

🔒
9. Children's Privacy

OneTraxx is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at info@onetraxx.com and we will delete it promptly.

📝
10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will notify users via an in-app notice. The "Last updated" date at the bottom of this page always reflects the most recent version.

Continued use of the app after changes are posted means you accept the updated policy.

Questions about this policy, data deletion requests, or anything else privacy-related? We aim to respond within 48 hours.
